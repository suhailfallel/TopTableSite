The TOPTABLE Vi20 PRO
has the following key features

Menu Customization

Menu customization allows restaurant owners and managers to easily update and tailor their menus to meet changing customer preferences and seasonal offerings.
Learn More

Seamless Table Management

This feature allows restaurant staff to efficiently manage table assignments, reservations, and guest seating. It provides a visual representation indicating which tables are occupied, reserved, or available.
Learn More

Inventory Control

Inventory control is essential for tracking and managing all ingredients, supplies, and goods used in the restaurant. The POS software helps monitor stock levels in real-time.
Learn More

Efficient Order Management

Efficient order management is crucial for accuracy and speed in a restaurant. It involves the process of taking orders, modifying them (e.g., special requests or dietary restrictions), and sending them to the kitchen or bar for preparation.
Learn More

Real-time Reporting

Real-time reporting generates comprehensive reports on various aspects of restaurant operations, including sales, inventory levels, employee performance, and customer data.Managers and owners can make informed decisions based on up-to-the-minute data.
Learn More

Staff Performance Tracking

This feature allows managers to monitor and evaluate employee performance, including sales performance, attendance, and customer service
Learn More

Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

CRM tools within the POS system help manage customer data and interactions. It tracks customer preferences, contact information, and order history.
Learn More

Offline Functionality

Offline functionality ensures that the POS system can continue to operate, record transactions, and take orders even when the internet connection is temporarily lost.
Learn More

Premium Features

Recipe Management

Recipe management within a POS system involves storing and organizing recipes for dishes served in the restaurant. It helps to maintain consistency in food preparation and ensures that the culinary team follows standardized recipes for each menu item.

Nutrition Fact Integration

Integration of nutrition facts provides customers with essential dietary information about menu items, such as calorie counts, allergen information, and nutritional content.It caters to customers' health-conscious choices and dietary restrictions.

Digital Menu

A digital menu replaces traditional paper menus with digital displays or mobile apps. Customers can browse the menu, view images and descriptions of dishes, and place orders electronically. It enhances the dining experience.

Landline Caller ID Integration

Landline Caller ID Integration identifies incoming calls from customers placing orders through the restaurant's landline phone. Landline Caller ID Integration streamlines the order-taking process and ensures accuracy.

Cloud Integration

Cloud integration stores POS data in the cloud, enabling remote access to the system and data from anywhere with an internet connection. Cloud Integration enhances scalability, data backup, and facilitates off-site management.

Loyalty Programs

Loyalty programs reward repeat customers with discounts, free items, or other incentives. The POS system tracks customer loyalty points and rewards. Loyalty Programs encourages customer retention and repeat business.

Security and Data Protection

Security and data protection features safeguard sensitive customer and financial data. This includes encryption of payment information, user access controls, and compliance with data protection regulations.
